About us

Sheikh motors limited

This is a company that was started in 1995 in the backyard of the director Mr Suleiman Sheikh.

The director single handedly used to repair vehicle engines,upgrade engines and also do major and minor tune ups for sporting vehicles.The company grew from there recruiting employees as the demand increased

In 1997 the company took part as the rally crew for team Moughal which went on to come 6th in the Rhino Charge challenge.This win made enough capital for the company to move their garage to Ngong road where they increasingly and confidently built their name in Kenyan automotive markets

In 2015 due to public demand the Company director took the initiative to include Vehicle renting/hire as part of the company profile,to enable kenyan citizens have ease in their day to day activities
